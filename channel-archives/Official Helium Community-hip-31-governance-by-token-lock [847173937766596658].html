Tushar 05/26/2021 4:31 PM
The feedback on this HIP has been really helpful. After talking to several people I think I should modify this proposal to lock up tokens for an extended period rather than burn them. Long lockups achieve the same filter of only committed long term actors voting, and have the added benefit of forcing the voters to live with the consequences of their vote. Also, burning tokens is much scarier to people than I initially realized when drafting this proposal. Lockups seem less scary. High level summary of updated proposal: Whenever a vote is held, the winners tokens are locked up. The lockup is based on how contentious the vote was. If a vote passed with 95% or more, the winner's tokens don't get any additional lockup because we do not want to penalize changes that everyone wants to make. If a vote passes with 51% the lockup then the lockup should be several years so the voters are committed to the consequences of their contentious victory. I'd love to hear more input on this updated idea and will then incorporate it into the HIP (edited)
